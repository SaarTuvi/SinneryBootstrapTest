.WE ARE THE ANTI TRIBE.

Forged by the love for metal and hatred for anything else, Sinnery formed in late 2012 in Herzliya Israel.
The 4-piece toure Israel nonstop while entering the studio with Israeli producer Eli Pikover (Sonne Adam, Whorecore, Shredhead) to record their first album.

In 2016 the band signed with Pitch Black Records and together released Sinnery's debut LP "A Feast of Fools", getting the band to play in Cyprus's biggest metal festival "Power of The Night Fest 2017".
AFOF gained the band many new listeners receiving great responses worldwide.

In 2019 the band toured with legendary German thrashers Tankard.
Nowadays Sinnery finished their highly anticipated new album "Black Bile", produced by Yuval Kramer ( Hammercult, Amaseffer, Reign of The Architect) and mixed and mastered by Matt Hyde (Machine Head, Trivium, Slipknot, Bullet For My Valentine).
Sinnery is now looking for partners that can contribute to the release of their Black Bile album.
